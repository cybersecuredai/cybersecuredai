1. General Refund Principles

1.1 Our Commitment

  • Customer Satisfaction: We stand behind our services and want customers to be satisfied
  • Fair Treatment: All refund requests are evaluated fairly and consistently
  • Clear Terms: Transparent refund conditions and timelines
  • Prompt Processing: Quick processing of valid refund requests

1.2 Refund Eligibility

Refunds are available under the following conditions:

  • Service Issues: Significant service outages or performance problems
  • Billing Errors: Incorrect charges or unauthorized billing
  • Early Termination: Cancellation within specified timeframes
  • Special Circumstances: Evaluated on a case-by-case basis

2.1 Monthly Subscriptions

  • Refund Period: 14 days from billing date for first month
  • Cancellation: Can cancel anytime, effective end of current billing period
  • Prorated Refunds: Generally not available except for service issues
  • Auto-Renewal: Must cancel before next billing cycle

2.2 Annual Subscriptions

  • Refund Period: 30 days from initial billing for full refund
  • Partial Refunds: Prorated refunds available up to 90 days
  • Service Credits: May offer service credits instead of cash refunds
  • Early Termination: Subject to early termination fees after 90 days

3. Professional Services Refunds

3.1 Consulting and Implementation

  • Pre-Work Refunds: Full refund if cancelled before work begins
  • Work in Progress: Refund of unperformed work only
  • Completed Work: No refunds for completed deliverables
  • Milestone-Based: Refunds calculated based on milestone completion

3.2 Training and Certification

  • Advance Cancellation: Full refund if cancelled 7+ days before training
  • Short Notice: 50% refund if cancelled 3-6 days before training
  • Late Cancellation: No refund if cancelled less than 3 days before
  • No-Show Policy: No refund for no-shows without advance notice

3.3 Custom Development

  • Specification Phase: Full refund if cancelled during requirements gathering
  • Development Phase: Refund of remaining unperformed work
  • Delivery Phase: No refunds after code delivery and acceptance
  • Change Orders: Additional work is non-refundable once approved

4. Service-Level Based Refunds

4.1 SLA Breach Credits

  • Availability Credits: Automatic credits for uptime SLA breaches
  • Performance Credits: Credits for sustained performance issues
  • Response Time Credits: Credits for missed response time commitments
  • Credit Calculation: Based on percentage of SLA breach severity

4.2 Service Disruptions

  • Extended Outages: Prorated refunds for outages exceeding SLA limits
  • Data Loss: Refunds and restoration costs for service-caused data loss
  • Security Incidents: Credits for service-caused security incidents
  • Migration Issues: Full refunds if migration services fail to deliver

5. Cancellation Procedures

5.1 Self-Service Cancellation

  • Account Portal: Cancel subscriptions through customer portal
  • Immediate Effect: Cancellation effective at end of current billing period
  • Confirmation: Email confirmation of cancellation sent immediately
  • Data Access: Continued access until end of billing period

5.2 Assisted Cancellation

  • Support Request: Contact support for cancellation assistance
  • Enterprise Accounts: Account manager assistance for complex cancellations
  • Government Accounts: Contract specialist support for federal cancellations
  • Educational Accounts: Academic calendar coordination for school cancellations

5.3 Involuntary Cancellation

  • Non-Payment: Service cancelled for non-payment after grace period
  • Policy Violations: Service terminated for acceptable use policy violations
  • Fraud: Immediate cancellation for fraudulent activity
  • Security Issues: Temporary suspension for security concerns

6. Data Handling After Cancellation

6.1 Data Retention Period

  • Grace Period: 30-day grace period for data retrieval after cancellation
  • Export Options: Data export tools available during retention period
  • Format Options: Multiple export formats available (CSV, JSON, XML)
  • Migration Support: Assistance with data migration to other platforms

6.2 Data Deletion

  • Automated Deletion: Automatic secure deletion after retention period
  • Immediate Deletion: Available upon request for privacy reasons
  • Compliance Requirements: Legal and compliance data retained as required
  • Backup Removal: Data removed from all backups and archives

7.1 Government and Education

  • Appropriation Issues: Refunds available for loss of government funding
  • Budget Cuts: Prorated refunds for mid-year budget reductions
  • Regulatory Changes: Refunds if regulatory changes make service unusable
  • Academic Calendar: Flexible terms aligned with academic year

7.2 Extraordinary Circumstances

  • Force Majeure: Service credits for force majeure events
  • Natural Disasters: Flexible terms for customers affected by disasters
  • Economic Hardship: Case-by-case review for documented hardship
  • Company Closure: Prorated refunds if customer business closes

7.3 Service Changes

  • Material Changes: Refunds available for material service changes
  • Feature Removal: Credits if essential features are discontinued
  • Price Increases: Option to cancel without penalty for significant increases
  • Merger/Acquisition: Flexible terms during ownership changes

8. Refund Request Process

8.1 How to Request Refunds

  • Online Portal: Submit refund requests through customer portal
  • Email Request: Send detailed request to billing@cybersecuredai.com
  • Phone Support: Call customer support for urgent refund requests
  • Account Manager: Enterprise customers contact dedicated account manager

8.2 Required Information

  • Account Details: Account number, billing email, service details
  • Reason for Refund: Clear explanation of refund reason
  • Documentation: Supporting documentation when applicable
  • Preferred Method: Preferred refund method (credit card, check, credit)

8.3 Processing Timeline

  • Acknowledgment: Request acknowledged within 1 business day
  • Review Period: Refunds reviewed within 5 business days
  • Processing Time: Approved refunds processed within 7-10 business days
  • Complex Cases: Complex refunds may take up to 15 business days

9. Refund Methods and Limitations

9.1 Refund Methods

  • Original Payment Method: Refunds issued to original payment method when possible
  • Account Credits: Service credits may be offered as alternative
  • Check Payment: Paper checks available for expired cards or closed accounts
  • Wire Transfer: Available for international refunds or large amounts

9.2 Refund Limitations

  • Processing Fees: Credit card processing fees may not be refundable
  • Third-Party Costs: External service costs (licenses, integrations) non-refundable
  • Usage-Based Charges: Charges for actual resource usage generally non-refundable
  • Partial Months: Partial month refunds calculated on daily basis

10. Appeals and Escalation

10.1 Refund Appeals

  • Appeal Timeline: Appeals must be submitted within 30 days
  • Additional Documentation: Opportunity to provide additional supporting information
  • Management Review: Appeals reviewed by senior management

10.2 Executive Escalation

  • Executive Review: Complex cases escalated to executive team
  • Customer Advocacy: Dedicated customer advocacy team for dispute resolution
  • Mediation: Third-party mediation available for major disputes
  • Final Resolution: Executive decisions are final for refund matters
