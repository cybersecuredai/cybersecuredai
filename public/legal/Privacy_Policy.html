Privacy Policy
CyberSecured AI Enterprise Cybersecurity Platform
Effective Date: September 6, 2025
Important: This Privacy Policy describes how CyberSecured AI ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, processes, and protects your personal information when you use our cybersecurity platform and services, particularly for educational institutions and government organizations.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

  • Account registration data (name, email address, phone number)
  • Professional information (organization, job title, department)
  • Authentication credentials and multi-factor authentication data
  • Communication data (support tickets, feedback, correspondence)

1.2 Technical Information

  • IP addresses and geolocation data for threat analysis
  • Device information (browser type, operating system, device IDs)
  • Log data (access times, pages viewed, actions performed)
  • Security event data and threat intelligence indicators
  • Network traffic patterns and anomaly detection data

1.3 Organization Data

  • Network infrastructure information
  • Security configuration data
  • Compliance assessment results
  • Incident response data and forensic information

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Service Delivery

  • Providing cybersecurity monitoring and threat detection
  • Delivering real-time security alerts and notifications
  • Generating compliance reports and assessments
  • Supporting incident response and forensic analysis

2.2 Platform Enhancement

  • Improving AI-powered threat detection algorithms
  • Enhancing behavioral analytics and anomaly detection
  • Developing new security features and capabilities
  • Conducting security research and threat intelligence analysis

2.3 Legal and Compliance

  • Meeting regulatory requirements (FERPA, FISMA, GDPR, CCPA)
  • Supporting legal investigations and law enforcement requests
  • Maintaining audit trails and compliance documentation
  • Protecting against fraud, abuse, and security threats

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

3.1 We DO NOT Sell Personal Data

CyberSecured AI does not sell, rent, or trade personal information to third parties for commercial purposes.

3.2 Limited Sharing

  • Service Providers: Authorized vendors for hosting, analytics, and support services
  • Threat Intelligence: Anonymous threat indicators shared with security community
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or court order
  • Emergency Situations: To prevent imminent harm to individuals or organizations

3.3 Government and Education Specific

  • Student data shared only as permitted by FERPA and institutional agreements
  • Government data handled according to FedRAMP and FISMA requirements
  • Cross-border transfers comply with applicable data protection laws

4. Data Security and Protection

4.1 Technical Safeguards

  • End-to-end encryption for data in transit and at rest
  • Multi-factor authentication and zero-trust architecture
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Automated threat detection and incident response

4.2 Organizational Measures

  • Employee background checks and security training
  • Role-based access controls and principle of least privilege
  • Data classification and handling procedures
  • Business continuity and disaster recovery planning

4.3 Compliance Certifications

  • SOC 2 Type II compliance for security and availability
  • FedRAMP authorized cloud service offerings
  • ISO 27001 information security management
  • NIST Cybersecurity Framework alignment

5. Your Privacy Rights

5.1 General Rights

  • Access: Request copies of your personal data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request removal of your personal data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured format

5.2 GDPR Rights (EU Residents)

  • Right to object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to restrict processing in certain circumstances
  • Right to withdraw consent for consent-based processing
  • Right to lodge complaints with supervisory authorities

5.3 CCPA Rights (California Residents)

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of sale of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

6. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy:

  • Account Data: Duration of service relationship plus 7 years
  • Security Logs: 13 months for standard logs, 7 years for compliance
  • Incident Data: 7 years for forensic and legal purposes
  • Student Data: As specified in educational institution agreements
  • Government Data: According to federal records retention schedules

7. International Transfers

CyberSecured AI operates globally and may transfer data across borders. We ensure adequate protection through:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for EU transfers
  • Data Processing Agreements with international partners
  • Regular assessments of destination country privacy laws
  • Additional safeguards for government and education data

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are designed for organizations, not individual consumers. However, we recognize that educational institutions may handle student data:

  • We comply with COPPA for children under 13
  • Student data is processed only under school authority and consent
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children for commercial purposes
  • Educational institutions maintain primary responsibility for student privacy rights

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. When we make material changes:

  • We will notify users via email and platform notifications
  • Updates will be posted on our website with revision dates
  • Continued use of services constitutes acceptance of changes
  • For significant changes, we may request renewed consent
