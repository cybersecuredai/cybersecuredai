1. Overview and Purpose

This SMS Messaging Policy governs the use of text messaging services provided by CyberSecured AI platform, including security alerts, multi-factor authentication (MFA), and system notifications sent via SMS to our users' mobile devices.

Key Services: Security alerts, MFA codes, incident notifications, system status updates, and compliance reminders.

2. Consent and Opt-In Requirements

2.1 Express Consent

By providing your mobile phone number and agreeing to receive SMS messages, you provide express written consent to receive text messages from CyberSecured AI. This consent is required for:

  • Multi-factor authentication codes
  • Security incident alerts and warnings
  • System maintenance notifications
  • Compliance deadline reminders
  • Platform status updates

2.2 Double Opt-In Process

Upon initial registration, users will receive a confirmation SMS to verify their phone number. Users must respond with the requested confirmation code to activate SMS services.

2.3 Consent Documentation

We maintain records of your consent including:

  • Date and time of consent
  • Method of consent collection
  • IP address and user agent information
  • Specific services consented to

3. Types of SMS Messages

3.1 Security and Authentication Messages

Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA):

  • Time-sensitive verification codes (valid for 10 minutes)
  • Login attempt notifications
  • Account access confirmations
  • Password reset verification codes

Security Alerts:

  • Critical threat detections
  • Suspicious activity notifications
  • Security breach alerts
  • Vulnerability scan results (high/critical only)

3.2 System Notifications

  • Planned maintenance windows
  • System outage notifications
  • Service restoration confirmations
  • Critical platform updates

3.3 Compliance Notifications

  • Regulatory deadline reminders
  • Audit preparation notices
  • Compliance status updates
  • Required training notifications

4. Message Frequency and Timing

4.1 Message Volume

The frequency of SMS messages varies based on:

  • MFA Messages: As needed for authentication (average 2-10 per day)
  • Security Alerts: Based on threat activity (0-5 per day typically)
  • System Notifications: 1-2 per month for maintenance
  • Compliance Reminders: 2-4 per month during compliance periods

4.2 Delivery Hours

Messages are typically sent during business hours (6 AM - 10 PM local time), except for:

  • Critical security alerts (sent immediately)
  • MFA codes (sent immediately)
  • Emergency system notifications

5. Opt-Out and Unsubscribe Options

5.1 How to Opt-Out

You can stop receiving SMS messages at any time by:

  • Text STOP: Reply "STOP" to any SMS message
  • Account Settings: Disable SMS notifications in your user dashboard
  • Email Request: Send request to legal@cybersecuredai.com
  • Support Ticket: Submit request through our support system

5.2 Partial Opt-Out Options

Users can selectively disable specific message types while maintaining others:

  • Keep MFA codes but disable marketing messages
  • Receive critical alerts only
  • Enable business hours messaging only

Important: Disabling MFA SMS messages may require alternative authentication methods and could impact account security.

6. Data Protection and Privacy

6.1 Information Collection

We collect and process the following SMS-related data:

  • Mobile phone numbers
  • Message delivery status
  • Opt-in/opt-out preferences
  • Timestamp of consent and interactions
  • Carrier information (for delivery optimization)

6.2 Data Protection Measures

  • Phone numbers are encrypted at rest and in transit
  • Access is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Compliance with SOC 2 Type II standards

6.3 Data Sharing

We share SMS data only with:

  • Authorized SMS service providers (Twilio)
  • Mobile carriers (for message delivery)
  • Legal authorities when required by law

7. Technical Requirements and Limitations

7.1 Supported Carriers

Our SMS service supports all major U.S. carriers including AT&T, Verizon, T-Mobile, Sprint, and most regional carriers.

7.2 Message Delivery

While we strive for reliable delivery, SMS messages may be delayed or fail due to:

  • Network congestion
  • Carrier filtering
  • Device settings or limitations
  • Invalid phone numbers

7.3 Message and Data Rates

Standard message and data rates apply. CyberSecured AI does not charge for SMS services, but your mobile carrier's rates apply.

8. Security and MFA-Specific Policies

8.1 MFA Code Security

  • Codes are randomly generated and single-use only
  • Codes expire after 10 minutes
  • Maximum of 5 code requests per hour per user
  • Failed attempts are logged and may trigger security reviews

8.2 Security Alert Protocols

  • Critical alerts are sent immediately regardless of time
  • Alerts include threat level and recommended actions
  • Follow-up instructions provided via secure portal

9. Compliance and Legal Requirements

9.1 TCPA Compliance

We comply with the Telephone Consumer Protection Act by:

  • Obtaining express written consent before sending SMS
  • Providing clear opt-out mechanisms
  • Maintaining consent records
  • Honoring opt-out requests within 10 days

9.2 International Compliance

For international users, we comply with:

  • GDPR (European Union)
  • PIPEDA (Canada)
  • Local telecommunications regulations

10. Support and Help

For SMS-related support:

  • Text HELP: Reply "HELP" to any SMS message
  • Email Support: support@cybersecuredai.com
  • Phone Support: Available to Enterprise customers
  • Knowledge Base: Available in your user dashboard

11. Policy Changes and Updates

We may update this SMS Policy from time to time. Users will be notified of material changes through:

  • Email notification to registered users
  • SMS notification for policy updates affecting SMS services
  • Prominent notice on the platform
  • Updated "Last Modified" date
