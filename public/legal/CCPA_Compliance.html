4. Your California Privacy Rights

As a California resident, you have the following rights regarding your personal information:

Right Description How to Exercise
Right to Know Categories and specific pieces of personal information collected Email ccpa@cybersecuredai.com or call our privacy hotline
Right to Delete Request deletion of your personal information Online form, email, or phone request
Right to Correct Correct inaccurate personal information Account settings or contact customer support
Right to Opt-Out of Sale Opt-out of sale of personal information Not applicable - we do not sell personal information
Right to Opt-Out of Sharing Opt-out of sharing for targeted advertising Not applicable - we do not share for advertising
Right to Limit Sensitive PI Limit use of sensitive personal information Contact privacy team for applicable categories
Right to Non-Discrimination Equal treatment when exercising privacy rights Automatic - no action required

5. Exercising Your Rights

5.1 Submitting Requests

You can exercise your California privacy rights through the following methods:

  • Online Form: Available at cybersecuredai.com/privacy-request
  • Email: ccpa@cybersecuredai.com
  • Phone: 1-800-CYBER-AI (privacy hotline)
  • Mail: Privacy Team, 395 Pitchfork Trail Suite 902, Willow Park, TX 76087

5.2 Identity Verification

To protect your privacy, we will verify your identity before processing requests:

  • Account Holders: Login to your account for automatic verification
  • Non-Account Holders: Provide email address and answer verification questions
  • Sensitive Requests: May require additional documentation
  • Authorized Agents: Must provide written authorization and proof of agency

5.3 Response Timeline

  • Acknowledgment: Within 10 business days
  • Response: Within 45 days (extendable to 90 days for complex requests)
  • Free Service: Up to 2 requests per 12-month period at no charge
  • Format: Portable and readily usable format when applicable

6. Authorized Agents

6.1 Agent Requirements

You may designate an authorized agent to make privacy requests on your behalf. The agent must:

  • Be registered with the California Secretary of State (for businesses)
  • Provide written authorization from you
  • Verify their own identity
  • Confirm they are authorized to act on your behalf

6.2 Agent Verification

  • We may require the agent to submit proof of authorization
  • We may contact you directly to confirm the agent's authority
  • Power of attorney documentation may be required
  • Additional verification steps may apply for sensitive requests

7. Retention Periods

We retain personal information only as long as necessary for business purposes:

7.1 Standard Retention Periods

  • Customer Account Data: Duration of service plus 3 years
  • Transaction Records: 7 years for business and tax purposes
  • Marketing Data: Until opt-out or 3 years of inactivity
  • Security Logs: 13 months for operations, longer for compliance

7.2 Automatic Deletion

  • Automated systems delete data when retention periods expire
  • Users receive advance notice before account deletion
  • Data exports available before deletion when requested
  • Legal hold procedures override automated deletion when required

8. Non-Discrimination

8.1 Equal Treatment

CyberSecured AI will not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA rights. We will not:

  • Deny goods or services
  • Charge different prices or rates
  • Provide different quality of goods or services
  • Suggest you will receive different treatment

8.2 Permissible Differences

We may offer financial incentives or different services if:

  • The difference is reasonably related to the value of your personal information
  • We provide clear disclosure of material terms
  • You provide opt-in consent
  • You can withdraw from the program at any time
